About
A DIY enthusiast, Gabriela Salvador Thumé loves to experiment with needles, seam, craft, circuits and algorithms.

She is a Computer Science student at Santa Catarina State University and researched Free Hardware and Software Socialization as part of a scholarship extension program in Colmeia Group for one year and a half.

Also a member of MuSA Collective (Multimedia, Systems and Arts) where developed experiments on software and hardware, mainly related with wearable computing using FLOSS technologies like Processing and Pure Data.

Currently living in Guimarães, Portugal, attending in an internship on Computer Graphics Center, developping and researching multimidia virtual actors.
